About us

Independent coverage of Seedance & AI creative tools

Seedance-2 is an independent news and insights site focused on Seedance and the wider world of AI-powered creative tools. We cover product updates, feature launches, industry trends, and practical tips so readers can stay informed without wading through hype or scattered social posts.

What we do

Our team follows official announcements, developer notes, and community feedback to turn complex capabilities into clear, timely reporting. Whether you are a creator exploring video and media workflows, a professional evaluating new tooling, or simply curious about how AI is reshaping content production, we aim to be a reliable starting point for understanding what Seedance offers and how it fits into the broader landscape.

Editorial approach

We are not affiliated with Seedance’s creators unless we state otherwise; we summarize, contextualize, and analyze publicly available information. Accuracy matters to us: when we correct an article, we note it. We also welcome feedback and story ideas from readers who care about transparent, useful coverage of AI tools.

Seedance-2 is built for people who want straightforward news and analysis—not clickbait—about Seedance and the future of AI-assisted creation. Thank you for reading.
